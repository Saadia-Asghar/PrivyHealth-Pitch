WhatsApp Bot

Zero training.
Zero app download.

Pharmacists with basic Android phones send one WhatsApp message and receive full prescription details — no app to install, no QR scanner, no training required. Works on every phone in Pakistan.

verify [CODE]
Returns medicine name, dosage, doctor, expiry, and allergy alerts instantly
fill [CODE]
Marks prescription as dispensed — permanently locked on WireFluid against re-use
refill [CODE]
Checks remaining refill count and issues refill if authorised by doctor
my record
Returns a secure link to the patient's full digital health dashboard
emergency
Provides instant access to blood type, allergies, and emergency contacts
help
Lists all available commands in English and Urdu
📱 Works on any basic Android • No app install • Bi-lingual (English + Urdu)
